Lore

Deep beneath the crystal caves, the Mimu Vault was sealed to keep the Nulls from the world above. Only a living flame can hold them back.

You carry the last light. Four rock-crystal lanterns still burn in the dark—relight them, and the vault door will answer. Turn wrong, and the Nulls will find you first.

Carry the flame. Escape the vault. Do not let the dark follow you out.
